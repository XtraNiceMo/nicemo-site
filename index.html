About NiceMo LLC

NiceMo LLC is a Las Vegas-based clinical AI company founded to close the gap between emerging AI technology and the complex realities of long-term care.

Every tool we build is grounded in a single principle: certifiable, source-cited information only. We do not fabricate claims, invent certifications, or add aspirational features that do not exist.

Our team brings experience in clinical operations, CMS regulatory compliance, and AI development, working directly with Directors of Nursing to build tools that fit real workflows.
